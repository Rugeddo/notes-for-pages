# Notes for Pages — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

## Data Collection
Notes for Pages does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## Data Storage
All notes and checklists are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data leaves your browser.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.
